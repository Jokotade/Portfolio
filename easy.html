Easy

Easy is a crypto to fiat currency swap application tackling the predominant obstacle to cryptocurrency adoption in Nigeria by facilitating efficient and fluid exchange between traditional fiat currencies and cryptocurrencies.

We raised Series A funding, following a successful presentation of our user interface and solution to key investors.

Role

Senior Product Designer, I drive the designing of innovative, user-centric currency swap application, leveraging thorough research and analysis to inform design decisions.

Timeline

8 Weeks

Skills & Method

UX Research, Information Architecture, Qualitative research, User interviews, Usability testing, UX design, UI design, and Quantitative research

1 Product Designer, 1 Product Owner, 1 Product Manager and 6 Developers

Tools

Figma, Miro, Zeplin, Notion, Survey monkey, Maze and UserZoom

Target Audience

Crypto traders comprises of tech-savvy agent and customer.

Project Overview

The challenge:

Nigeria is used as a case study where people find it difficult to withdraw and deposit funds in the crypto ecosystem with government policies making it harder and with peer to peer is risky only a few trusted people are available.

Project Goals:

To create On/Off ramp system which will be a solution to the biggest challenge of cryptocurrency adoption in Nigeria the goal is to make money flow between the traditional (fiat) and crypto ecosystems.

Target Users:

Nigerian Tech-savvy crypto traders, age 16 - 50 years comprises of agent that help the customer exchange their cryptocurrency to cash and vice versa and customer who approach the agents to help in currency swap.

Design Process

Empathize

To start the process we have to deeply understand the people we are designing for by putting ourselves in their shoes to carryout a user research.

User Research

To understand the user needs and pain points we conducted research on the customer and agent using user interviews, and we also do competitor analysis to understand our competitor.

User Interview

A semi-structured interview was conducted, below are the question for the interview.

Agent
  • What is your understanding of cryptocurrencies?
  • What type of cryptocurrencies do you know?
  • Do you have crypto wallet and what currencies?
  • What app do you use to trade?
  • Do you have smartphone, and what type?
  • If given an application can you swap currencies for customers?
  • Will there be any barrier stoping you from using the provided application?
Customer
  • What cryptocurrencies do you trade with?
  • How do you put money in your crypto wallet?
  • How do you withdraw your money from crypto wallet?
  • Which application do you use to trade?
  • Do you have any challenge withdrawing your money from crypto wallet?
  • What better way do you think you can deposit and withdraw from your crypto wallet?

Ideate

The transition from problem definition to solution generation was facilitated through focused brainstorming ideation sessions.The ideation phase yielded a multitude of innovative solutions. We use the post-it voting to pick best idea from the generated form brainstorm session, we voted the idea that best solve the crypto to fiat current currency swap problem.

Solutions

  • Provide a mobile application to be used by people who help in currency exchange called an agent.
  • A platform where people can able to convert crypto to fiat and vice versa as easily as possible.
  • Providing a secure and trusted application for both agents and customers.
  • An agent can fund a wallet with supported currencies.
  • An agent can withdraw the same currencies they deposit.
  • An agent can generate revenue by setting up the commission.
  • A platform that is independent of government policies.
  • An agent should be able to swap currencies both online and offline to their customer.
  • A platform that accepts the use of cash and bank transfers for fiat payment.
  • The support cryptocurrencies will be bitcoin, Ethereum, litecoin, USDC, and USDT and fiat is Naira.

Test & Iteration

Usability Testing

Upon completing the UI design, we developed a clickable prototype and conducted usability testing with five participants to assess design effectiveness and identify potential enhancements.

The following enhancements were implemented:

  • Set up Commission: We set up a fix commission percentage that agent will charge their customer, the agent preferred to be the one to setup the commission percentage they will charge their customer so we allow them to do that but will still but a limit to amount they can charge the customer so that they wont overcharge them too.
  • Improving Withdrawal - The agent ask for an option to withdraw the crypto on their wallet to their bank account then we added the option to covert their crypto wallet to fiat to able to withdraw to their verify bank account.
